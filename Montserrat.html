Montserrat

Quick Facts on Montserrat
Capital Brades (de facto) (Population: 300+ to 400+); Plymouth (de jure); Little Bay (future [potential] capital city)
Location Caribbean Sea ('Lesser Antilles')
Overall Land Size About 39 square miles (rough estimate)
Population 5,148 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population between 5,100+ and 5,200+ between 2014 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1632
[Official] Motto "A people of excellence, moulded by nature, nurtured by God"
