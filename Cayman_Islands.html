Cayman Islands

Quick Facts on the Cayman Islands
Capital George Town ( Population: 29,370 (rough estimate)) on Grand Cayman Island
Location Western Caribbean Sea ('Greater Antilles'')
Overall Land Size 102 square miles (rough estimate); this land size includes the three islands that makes up the Cayman Islands: Grand Cayman (the largest and most populous of the three) and its 'sister islands,' Cayman Brac and Little Cayman.
Population 63,258 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population anywhere between 54,000+ and 60,000+ between 2013 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1962 (was originally part of the colony of Jamaica after Spain ceded these lands in 1670); Jamaica chose to become an independent [Commonwealth] nation in that same year.
[Official] Motto "He hath founded it upon the seas"
