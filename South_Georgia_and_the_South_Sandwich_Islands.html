South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands

Quick Facts on the South Georgia and South Sandwich Islands
Capital (Research Station) King Edward Point
Location South Atlantic Ocean (about 620+ miles from the Falkland Islands)
Overall Land Size 1,570 square miles (rough estimate); this includes both the [main] South Georgia Island (and every nearby islets/rocks and islands) and the South Sandwich Islands (from the northern Traversay Islands to the 'Southern Thule') further southeast (click here for more information).
Population Less than 100 (Varies between seasons; South Georgia Island [only]) (no indigenous population); Uninhabited (South Sandwich Islands) (also click on this link for more information)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1843 (South Georgia Island); 1908 (South Sandwich Islands) (both were discovered by James Cook in 1775)
[Official] Motto Leo terram propriam protegat (Latin: "Let the lion protect his own land")
