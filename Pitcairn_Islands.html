Pitcairn Islands

Quick Facts on the Pitcairn Islands
Capital Adamstown (the only inhabited area in the territory)
Location Southern Pacific Ocean
Overall Land Size 18.1 square miles (rough estimate; aside from the main 'Pitcairn Island,' this overall landsize includes the following islands of the archipalego: Ducie Island (about 220 miles east of 'Henderson Island'), Henderson Island (about 120 miles northeast of the 'main island'), and Oeno Island (about 89 miles northwest of the 'main island'))
Population About 50 (other online sources have placed the total population anywhere between 40+ and 60+)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1838; the three uninhabited islands were annexed into the territory in 1938
[Official] Motto N/A
