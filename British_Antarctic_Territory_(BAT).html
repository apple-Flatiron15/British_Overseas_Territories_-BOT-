British Antaractic Territory

Quick Facts on the British Antarctic Territory
Capital (Main Base) Rothera Research Station
Location Antarctica (near Chile and Argentina)
Overall Land Size 666,000 square miles (rough estimate)
Population Varies between the winter and summer seasons (ranging between double-digit (winter) and triple-digit (summer) numbers; no indigenous population); population mostly consists of scientists, researchers, crew members of sea-faring vessels, field-workers, and their support staffs.
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1908 (check on the link in the 'Overall Land Size' row above)
[Official] Motto Research and discovery
