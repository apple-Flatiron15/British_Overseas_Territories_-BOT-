Bermuda

Quick Facts on Bermuda
Capital Hamilton (Population: About 1,800 (rough estimate))
Location North Atlantic Ocean; about 650+ miles from Buxton, North Carolina (the closest town/city to the territory)
Overall Land Size 20.54 square miles (rough estimate; Google query)
Population 66,433 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population anywhere between 61,000+ and 70,000+ between 2013 and 2016) (the largest population out of all of the 'British Overseas Territories')
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1684 (first settled in 1609)
[Official] Motto "Quo fata ferunt" (Latin: "Whither the Fates carry [us]")
