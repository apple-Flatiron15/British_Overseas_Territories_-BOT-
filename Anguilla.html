Anguilla

Quick Facts on Anguilla
Capital The Valley (Population: About 2,000+ (2011 estimate))
Location Caribbean Sea ('Lesser Antilles')
Overall Land Size 35 square miles (rough estimate); this land size includes a number of adjacent islands, islets, and cays both northwest, northeast, and (slightly) southwest of the main island of Anguilla.
Population 16,866 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population anywhere between 13,000+ and 16,000+ between 2013 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1980 (originally part of St Kitts and Nevis (currently a British Commonwealth Nation))
[Official] Motto "Strength and Endurance"
