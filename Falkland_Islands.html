Quick Facts on the Falkland Islands
Capital Stanley (Population: 2,000+ to 2,100+)
Location South Atlantic Ocean
Overall Land Size 4,700 square miles (rough estimate)
Population 2,840 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that place the current population between 2,800+ and 2,900+ between 2015 and 2016) (this population includes [foreign] civilian contractors and British military personnels stationed on the islands)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1841; 1833 (re-established control over the islands from the Argentines)
[Official] Motto Desire the Right
