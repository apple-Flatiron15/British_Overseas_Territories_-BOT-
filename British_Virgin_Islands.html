British Virgin Islands

Quick Facts on the British Virgin Islands
Capital Road Town
Location Caribbean Sea ('Lesser Antilles')
Overall Land Size 59 square miles (rough estimate)
Population 27,755 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population anywhere between 30,000+ and 33,000+ between 2014 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1960 ([greater] autonomy status for the islands was established in 1967)
[Official] Motto "Vigilate" (Latin: "Be watchful")
