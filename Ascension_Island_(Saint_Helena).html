Quick Facts on the Ascension Island
Capital Georgetown (Population: About 450+ (2008))
Location South Atlantic Ocean (about 700 miles northwest of the island of Saint Helena (check the link below in the next row))
Overall Land Size 34 square miles (rough estimate)
Population Varies (according to various online sources, the population could range anywhere between 800+ to 1,100+, since the year 2010); the population consists of mostly citizens from the island of Saint Helena, as well as a number of British and American civilian and military personnels (no indigenous population)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1922 (see 'Preamble - subsection (d)')
