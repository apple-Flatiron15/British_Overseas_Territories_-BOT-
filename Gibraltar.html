Gibraltar

Quick Facts on Gibraltar
Capital N/A (Gibraltar is one whole city, due to its small [land] size)
Location Iberian Peninsula (Southern Spain)
Overall Land Size 2.6 square miles (rough estimate)
Population 29,868 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that place the current population between 29,000+ and 32,000+ between 2015 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1830; 1713 (Treaty of Utrecht; ceded by Spain)
[Official] Motto "Nulli expugnabilis hosti" (Latin: "No enemy shall expel us"); (on its 'Coat of Arms') - "Montis Insignia Calpe" (Latin: "Badge of the Rock of Gibraltar")
