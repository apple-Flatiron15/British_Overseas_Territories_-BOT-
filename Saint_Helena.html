Quick Facts on Saint Helena
Capital Jamestown (Population: 600+ to 700+)
Location South Atlantic Ocean
Overall Land Size 47 square miles (rough estimate)
Population 4,134 (2016; subject to change) (there are other [online] sources that have placed the current population anywhere between 4,000+ and 4,200+ between 2015 and 2016)
Year Established as a British 'crown colony' 1834 (see 'Preamble - subsection (b)')
[Official] Motto Loyal and Unshakeable
